DEFINITIONs

Chess is a game of two players played on a sixty-four square board, with alternating dark and light squares. Before the game starts, each player has 16 pieces all of one color, but different from the opponent's pieces. Black and white colors are prefered. The person playing with the white pieces is referred to as "white" and the opponent, "black".
